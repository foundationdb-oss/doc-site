## Why FoundationDB?

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

- :material-shield-check:{ .lg .middle } **ACID Transactions**

    ---

    Full transactional support across the entire database. Multi-key, multi-range transactions with serializable isolation—no compromises.

- :material-server-network:{ .lg .middle } **Distributed by Design**

    ---

    Scales horizontally across clusters of commodity servers. Add nodes to increase capacity without application changes.

- :material-lightning-bolt:{ .lg .middle } **High Performance**

    ---

    Millions of operations per second with single-digit millisecond latencies. Optimized for both read and write-heavy workloads.

- :material-shield-refresh:{ .lg .middle } **Fault Tolerant**

    ---

    Automatic failover and recovery. Your data survives hardware failures, network partitions, and even datacenter outages.

- :material-layers-triple:{ .lg .middle } **Multi-Model**

    ---

    Build any data model on top of the key-value foundation. Documents, graphs, tables, queues—your choice.

- :material-source-branch:{ .lg .middle } **Open Source**

    ---

    Apache 2.0 licensed. Battle-tested at Apple scale, available for everyone. Join a vibrant community of contributors.

</div>
